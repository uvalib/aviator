The Aviator: Remembering James Rogers McConnell

An exhibition commemorating the centennial of the death of James Rogers McConnell, the first of sixty-four men from the University of Virginia to give their lives in “the war to end all wars.” From the holdings of the Albert and Shirley Small Special Collections Library

March 6, 2017 - May 30, 2017

First Floor Gallery, Harrison Institute and
Small Special Collections Library, University of Virginia
